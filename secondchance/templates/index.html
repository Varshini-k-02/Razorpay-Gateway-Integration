ABOUT US

Second Chance is a small organisation that is envisioned in bringing into reality of the forgetton,buried dreams. There is a spark of inspiration in all employees who come forward in spreading the word.

Using this opportunity people have the necessary tools to nuture the minds of the young. We are currently limited to Chennai and we conduct out-reach programs every week. The feeling of seeing a kid working hard in such a young age is heart breaking. We reachout to the parents and convince them telling the importance of Education.


SERVICES

The money that is donated is used for enrolling the children in a nearby school. We make sure to buy all the necessary books and stationaries for the chlidren.
The students are carefully indentified on the basis of their family income and parent's occupation. During this pandemic,many lower middle class families have contacted us for help.
